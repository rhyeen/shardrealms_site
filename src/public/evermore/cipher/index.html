Evermore Cipher


If the message is encoded with the cipher symbols, use the keyboard below to type the decoded letter into the input above.


Letters can be entered as-is. Numbers should be separated by spaces.

For example, try:

  • Input: "lslclu aoyll", then click "Decode Letters" and look at result #19
  • Input: "17 15 18 2", then click "Decode Numbers" and look at result #2
